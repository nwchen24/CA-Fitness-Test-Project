California Standardized Testing for Disadvantaged Students

About the Data

The data used to create this visualization were collected from the California department of education. Pooled data for 2014 - 2016 was used for this visualization. This analysis focuses on FITNESSGRAM administered to 9th grade students and standardized tests administered to 10th grade students.

For each school, academic scores represent the mean score across all tests with available scores for that school. At the county level, the mean test score is calculated as the average of the mean score for each school in the county weighted by the number of students tested at each school.

The proportion of students at the school and county level in 'unhealthy' fitness zones is calculated as the total share of students deemed 'Needs Improvement' during administartion of the FITNESSGRAM.

More information about the underlying data can be found here: FITNESSGRAM ; Standardized Testing
